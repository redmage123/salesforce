1

📥 Instant Intake & Analysis

Customer message arrives → AI immediately analyzes:

  • Sentiment: Frustrated, urgent, churn risk detected

🤖 Autonomous Actions

💎 Value: Issue resolved in 2.3 seconds vs 10+ minutes manually

🔮 Proactive Health Monitoring

AI evaluates customer health in real-time:

  • Auto-Escalation: Alerts CSM + schedules retention call
💰 Value: Saves $127K account by triggering intervention BEFORE churn
5

📊 Knowledge Retrieval + Generation

AI crafts the perfect response:

  • Searches: 10 knowledge base articles in 0.3 seconds

6

✅ Resolution + Learning

AI closes the loop:

  • Sends Response: Agent reviews & sends (or auto-sends for simple cases)

  • Learns: Stores interaction patterns to improve future responses
🔄 Value: Continuous improvement - AI gets smarter with every case

AI approach: Instant analysis → Autonomous actions → Proactive escalation → Perfect response
