AI-generated contract improvements to reduce risk and optimize terms

HIGH PRIORITY Risk Impact: -0.8 points
Add Liability Cap at 12 Months of Fees

Current contract has unlimited liability exposure. Industry standard caps liability at 12 months of fees ($300K). This protects both parties from catastrophic losses.

📍 Suggested Section: Add new Section 6.3 "Limitation of Liability"
🎯 Expected Outcome: Reduces overall contract risk from 6.5 to 5.7
HIGH PRIORITY Risk Impact: -0.6 points
Reduce Payment Terms to Net 15

Net 30 payment terms increase cash flow risk. Negotiate to Net 15 (industry standard) to improve working capital and reduce accounts receivable risk.

📍 Suggested Section: Modify Section 3.2 "Payment Schedule"
🎯 Expected Outcome: Reduces payment default risk, improves cash flow by ~50%
MEDIUM PRIORITY Risk Impact: -0.4 points
Add Force Majeure Clause

Include standard force majeure protection against unforeseeable events (pandemics, natural disasters, war). Protects both parties from performance obligations during extraordinary circumstances.

📍 Suggested Section: Add new Section 8 "Force Majeure"
🎯 Expected Outcome: Provides legal protection against unforeseen events
MEDIUM PRIORITY Risk Impact: -0.3 points
Add GDPR/CCPA Compliance Section

Cloud services handling customer data require explicit data privacy terms. Include GDPR (EU) and CCPA (California) compliance requirements to avoid regulatory penalties.

📍 Suggested Section: Add new Section 9 "Data Privacy and Security"
🎯 Expected Outcome: Ensures compliance, avoids potential $250K-$2M fines
LOW PRIORITY Risk Impact: -0.2 points
Clarify IP Ownership Terms

Add explicit intellectual property ownership clause. Specify that work product created under this agreement belongs to the client (standard practice for service agreements).

📍 Suggested Section: Add new Section 7 "Intellectual Property"
🎯 Expected Outcome: Clarifies ownership, avoids disputes
LOW PRIORITY Risk Impact: -0.1 points
Extend Termination Notice Period

Consider increasing termination notice from 90 to 180 days for a 3-year contract. Provides more stability and allows adequate transition planning for both parties.

📍 Suggested Section: Modify Section 5 "Term and Termination"
🎯 Expected Outcome: Reduces sudden termination risk
